Stretch Routine

Stretching, like warm-ups and cooldowns are as important as your actual workout. Stretching improves your body's ability to move, boost flexibility, and reduce injury risk. Doing full-body stretches can also counteract the stress of exercise and give you greater power.

Lunges

Lunges are a popular strength training exercise among people wanting to strengthen and tone their bodies, while also improving overall fitness and enhancing athletic performance. This exercises does not require equipment, but a floor mat is recommended.

Push-Ups

Push-ups is a common calisthenics exercise. Traditional pushups are beneficial for building upper body strength. By raising and lowering the body using arms, push-ups exercise the pectoral muscles, and triceps. No equipment is needed, but a floor mat is recommended.
